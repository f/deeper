Features

Everything about your chats, visualized

Deeper connects to your local Beeper Desktop instance and turns your messaging data into beautiful, interactive analytics.

Dashboard

At-a-glance stats, connection categories, interactive hourly activity charts per platform, and a full platform ranking.

People

Cross-platform contact merging, ghosting detection, per-person response times, AI conversation summaries, and per-person word analytics.

Groups

Most active groups ranked by message volume, largest groups by members, per-platform breakdowns with unread and muted indicators.

Platforms

Chat distribution donut chart, groups vs DMs breakdown per platform, and detailed platform cards with top contacts.

Phrases

Word frequency analysis, word cloud, total and unique word counts — with date range filtering by week, month, quarter, or all time.

Response Time

See who responds fastest and who keeps you waiting. Per-person average reply times for both sides of the conversation.

Reels Leaderboard

See who you exchange the most Instagram Reels with. Sent vs received charts and summary stats with date range filtering.

Apple Intelligence (On-Device)

Conversation summaries powered by Apple's Foundation Models, running entirely on your Apple Silicon. No cloud, no API keys — your messages never leave your Mac.

Liquid Glass UI

Built with SwiftUI and native macOS 26 Tahoe design language, featuring .glassEffect() throughout.

Supported Platforms

All your chats, one dashboard

Get Started

Up and running in minutes

Connect Deeper to your Beeper Desktop in a few simple steps.

1

Open Beeper Desktop

Make sure Beeper Desktop is running on your Mac. Go to Settings.

2

Enable the Developer API

Navigate to the Developers tab in the sidebar. Toggle Beeper Desktop API to On. Enable Start on launch.

3

Generate an API Token

Scroll to Approved Connections and click the + button on the right to create a new token.

4

Launch Deeper

Open Deeper and click Connect Beeper Desktop API. Your token is stored securely in macOS Keychain.

5

Explore your data

Deeper fetches all your chats, merges contacts across platforms, analyzes messages, and presents interactive dashboards.

Privacy

Your data never leaves your machine

Deeper runs entirely locally. No servers, no telemetry, no tracking.

100% Local

Connects only to localhost:23373. AI summaries run on-device via Apple Silicon — nothing is ever sent to the cloud.

No Telemetry

No analytics, no tracking, no external network requests whatsoever.

Keychain Storage

Your Beeper auth token is stored in macOS Keychain, never in plaintext.
